Convert

Many people convert to Jesus these days. They look for a savior, to rescue them from this wicked world we live in. If you compare a piece of rotting meat, with the sin and wickedness of this world, they both share a process that escalates, the more time that passes the worse it gets. And it is really bad today.

Sin & Forgiveness

Christians often sound like they are judging themselves, and others. That's because they let Jesus / God judge them, when they repent from something. To be able to repent, there needs to be someone to pay the price for trespassing God's laws. Jesus is the perfect man. 100% human, and 100% God in one and the same being. It is the Holy Spirit that convicts us, and Jesus the spotless Lamb of God, that saves us from sin. It says in the Bible, If we confess our sins to Jesus, He is faithful and just, to forgive us our sins, and cleanse us from all unrightrightness. 1. John 1:9

Blood & Heart

Only Jesus' blood can cleanse us, when we repent of our sins from the bottom of our heart. Jesus said I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father, but by Me. John 14:6. You can't fool around, when it comes to repenting. You have to truly be sorry for what you have done wrong, and you need to turn 180 degrees, and go the other way. It requires action, and determination to repent, ask for forgiveness, and be cleansed.
